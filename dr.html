About Us

Dr. Shweta Diwan

With over 10 years of experience in nutrition and dietetics, Dr. Shweta Diwan is dedicated to helping people transform their lives through the power of proper nutrition.

After completing her Ph.D. in Nutritional Sciences, Dr. Diwan founded The HealthMechanix with a mission to provide customized nutrition plans that work with your body's unique needs.

Dr. Diwan believes that nutrition is not just about weight management but about creating a foundation for overall health and wellbeing. Her holistic approach combines cutting-edge nutritional science with practical, sustainable lifestyle changes.
